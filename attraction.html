Attraction places in Egypt

The Three Pyramids

The Giza pyramid complex (Arabic: مجمع أهرامات الجيزة), also called the Giza necropolis, is the site on the Giza Plateau in Greater Cairo, Egypt that includes the Great Pyramid of Giza, the Pyramid of Khafre, and the Pyramid of Menkaure, along with their associated pyramid complexes and the Great Sphinx of Giza.

Karnak Temple

Karnak temple is over 2,000 years old The Karnak, Egypt temple complex spans about 200 acres and was a work in progress for about 1,500 years.New Kingdom pharaohs such as Hatshepsut, Thutmose III, Seti I, and Ramses II made significant contributions to the complex during their reign.
